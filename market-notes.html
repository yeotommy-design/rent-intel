Market Notes

Short weekly notes on what changed in Singapore retail rent.

Market Notes is the Friday RentIntel release: one live rent pattern, one coverage update, and one practical decision cue so users can return quickly without reading a long report.

Area Watch Week of 16 May 2026

Tiong Bahru shophouse asking rent is still pressing above benchmark.

The asking layer is still ahead of the fair range, which means the real question is whether frontage, F&B approvals, and footfall support the premium or whether the landlord is simply pushing above a calmer benchmark.

Decision cue: keep the benchmark range visible in the conversation and ask for clearer comparable evidence before treating the premium as normal market movement.

Coverage Update Week of 16 May 2026

Heartland and fringe clusters now move into direct search faster.

RentIntel has added more direct-search coverage across heartland and fringe retail areas so users spend less time in the queue path and more time comparing benchmark versus asking rent immediately.

Decision cue: when a newly covered area appears, compare it against at least one nearby cluster before treating the first answer as a final pricing position.

Decision Cue Week of 16 May 2026

Renewal discussions still need range first, narrative second.

Even when landlords frame an increase as “market movement,” the safer order is still benchmark range, current gap, then unit-specific evidence. That keeps the conversation anchored to comparables instead of headline sentiment.

Decision cue: open Workspace when the discussion moves beyond the headline gap and into chart context, evidence source, and negotiation note territory.
