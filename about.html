About RentIntel

Singapore retail rent intelligence, not another listing portal.

RentIntel is designed to help tenants, operators, agents, and business owners understand whether a quoted retail rent looks fair. The product separates official transaction-backed rent from current asking-rent signals and labels each result by confidence.

Decision first

RentIntel turns fragmented rent data into a direct signal: under, fair, elevated, or high.

Source labeled

Official benchmarks, asking listings, geography, and manual classification are kept distinct.
