Disclaimer

RentIntel is decision support, not valuation or leasing advice.

Retail rent signals are working estimates based on the source layers available at the time of review. Official benchmark rent, asking-rent estimates, and confidence labels should help structure a decision, but they should not be treated as a guaranteed lease outcome.

Always verify: source data, lease terms, GST, service charge, permitted use, and handover condition before committing.

Negotiated rent may differ: asking-rent estimates can still diverge from the final agreed deal.

Confidence can move: thin listing volume, mixed-use properties, and shophouse classification can reduce certainty.
